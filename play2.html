MRS. ALLONBY.

_Au revoir_, Mr. Arbuthnot. Mind you bring me back something nice from your travels—not an Indian shawl—on no account an Indian shawl.

[_Exeunt_.]

GERALD

.

Mother, I have just written to him.

GERALD

Not marry him? Mother!

GERALD
.

To my father. I have written to tell him to come here at four o’clock this afternoon.
